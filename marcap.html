Critical Market Observations

  • Price erosion: Module prices hit record lows under $0.10/watt in some regions, forcing smaller Tier 2 manufacturers out of the market.
  • N-type dominance: By the end of 2025, N-type modules including TOPCon, HJT, and BC became the industry standard, making up over 85% of these shipments.
  • The gap: A significant volume gap remains between JA Solar at #4 and Tongwei at #5, separating the established Big Four from the rest of the industry.
